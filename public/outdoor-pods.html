Built for the Outdoors

Engineered to Withstand. Designed to Perform.

Every system inside the Backyard Silence Booth is purpose-built for outdoor environments where standard pods simply can't survive.

01
Grade III Waterproof

Carbon plastic composite panels, aviation-grade aluminium framing, and tempered glass with hermetic seals at every joint create a double anti-leaching structure against extreme weather.

02
Thermal Insulation

The carbon plastic composite panel resists water, UV, acid, salt, and corrosion with zero emission and fire resistance. Hollow glass construction delivers superior heat preservation year-round.

03
Integrated Air Conditioning

L and XL models feature a top-mounted integrated A/C — 2,500W cooling capacity, 350 m³/h airflow, R32 eco-friendly refrigerant. XXL uses a split household unit. Range: 15°C–30°C.

04
Low Voltage Protection

Battery consumption detection system prevents excessive discharge. When voltage hits the protection threshold the A/C auto-shuts down, requiring a manual restart once power recovers.

05
Smart Access Control

Enter via card, QR code, app control, or face scan. Surpasses 85% of market suppliers in management efficiency. Privacy curtains included as standard.

06
Modular Installation

Panel system follows a step-by-step sequence: floor → wall → glass → furniture. No heavy tools required on-site. Choose disassembled or assembled packing before production.
