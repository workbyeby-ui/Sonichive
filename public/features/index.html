Core Features

Six Pillars of Performance

Every pod ships with these six capabilities as standard -
no optional extras required.

30±5 dB Reduction

Advanced multi-layer acoustic panels absorb and block sound transmission. STC-rated wall assemblies ensure conversations inside stay inside, and noise outside stays out. Independently tested at Asia's largest acoustic lab.

Smart Ventilation System

Built-in active ventilation continuously circulates fresh air, regulating temperature and CO2 levels. Designed for sessions lasting hours, not the 15 minutes most pods accommodate before becoming uncomfortably stuffy.

Eco-Friendly Construction

Manufactured using recycled acoustic panels, low-emission materials, and energy-efficient LED lighting. SonicHive pods reduce environmental impact without compromising on acoustic performance or aesthetic quality.

Plug & Play Setup

Fully self-contained and modular with a uniform fastener system engineered using SolidWorks mechanics modelling. One power connection. No specialist tools. No contractors. Our team handles full setup.

7 Colour Options

Available in 7 standard colour finishes: Standard, Black, White, Red, Orange, Light Green, and Olive Green. Every finish is produced to the same premium standard, with no compromise on acoustic or build quality.

Privacy & Security

Lockable doors, privacy glass options, and acoustic-grade seals ensure complete confidentiality. Designed for legal, HR, financial, and executive conversations where privacy is not optional, it's essential.

Materials & Build

Built to Last

Every component is selected for acoustic performance, durability, and sustainability. No compromise.

Acoustic Panel Core

Multi-density recycled foam bonded to mass-loaded vinyl. Absorbs broadband frequencies, from low-frequency HVAC rumble to high-frequency speech.

Laminated Glass

3+3 tempered laminated soundproof glass in all window sections. Maintains visual openness while delivering acoustic isolation comparable to solid walls.

Acoustic Door Seals

Compression seals on all door edges create an airtight acoustic barrier when closed. The door is where most pods fail - ours are engineered to the same standard as the walls.

LED Lighting System

Reading spotlight and LED strip lighting at 4000K–5000K colour temperature. Optimised for focused work with no eye strain across extended sessions.
