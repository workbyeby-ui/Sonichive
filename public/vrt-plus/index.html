Fluid Modern Design

Extreme right-angle design with compact Japanese-style spacing. Fresh air inlet at top, outlet at bottom for a natural breeze feel.

Touchless NFC Entry

Induction switch replaces traditional buttons. NFC tap-to-enter for hygienic, keyless access — redefining "touch" for the modern workplace.

Panasonic centrifugal fan: 95 m³/hr at 1,200 RPM. Servo variable-speed motor with labyrinth ducts — whisper-quiet comfort at all times.
