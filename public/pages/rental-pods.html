FAQ

Rental Questions Answered

How far in advance do I need to book?+
We recommend enquiring at least 1–2 weeks before your event date to guarantee availability, especially for multiple units or events in peak season. For urgent same-week requests, contact us directly and we'll do our best to accommodate.
Which pod models are available for rental?+
All models from the SonicHive lineup are available for rental — Solo, Duo, Quartet, and Hexa Silence Booths. You can mix and match models in a single order. Custom branding is available for longer-term rentals.
Do rental pods have the same acoustic performance?+
Yes. Every rental pod delivers the same 30dB noise reduction as our purchased units. There is no compromise on acoustic performance for rental deployments — same panels, same seals, same ventilation.
How long does installation take at my venue?+
Installation time varies by model. A Solo Booth takes approximately 1 hour, while a Hexa takes around 2.5 hours. For multi-pod deployments, our team works in parallel — typical event setups of 4–6 pods are completed within a half-day.
Can I convert a rental into a purchase?+
Yes — and many of our clients do exactly this. If you'd like to purchase after your rental period, we apply a portion of your rental fee toward the purchase price. Ask about our rent-to-buy option when enquiring.
What if something goes wrong during the rental?+
Our support team is contactable throughout your rental period. For event deployments, on-site support can be arranged. All rental pods are covered under SonicHive's service guarantee — any equipment fault is our responsibility, not yours.
