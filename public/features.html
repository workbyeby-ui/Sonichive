Acoustic Science

The 30dB Difference

Sound reduction isn't a feature you can fake. Our pods are engineered with multi-density acoustic foam layers, mass-loaded vinyl barriers, and decoupled wall construction — the same techniques used in professional recording studios.

At 30dB of attenuation, a conversation at normal speaking volume inside the pod is reduced to below ambient noise levels outside. That means your confidential calls stay confidential — every time.

Every model is tested in a certified acoustic lab before production commences, and every shipment is quality-verified against SonicHive's acoustic performance standards.

Materials & Build

Built to Last

Every component is selected for acoustic performance, durability, and sustainability. No compromise.

Acoustic Panel Core

Multi-density recycled foam bonded to mass-loaded vinyl. Absorbs broadband frequencies — from low-frequency HVAC rumble to high-frequency speech.

Laminated Glass

Double-laminated acoustic glass panels in all window sections. Maintains visual openness while delivering acoustic isolation comparable to solid walls.

Acoustic Door Seals

Compression seals on all door edges create an airtight acoustic barrier when closed. The door is where most pods fail — ours are engineered to the same standard as the walls.

LED Lighting System

Dimmable, tunable white LED panels with colour temperature control. Adjustable from warm 2700K to cool 6500K to match any working preference or time of day.
