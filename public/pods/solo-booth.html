What's Inside

Everything You Need. Nothing You Don't.

Every Solo Booth ships with these features as standard. No optional extras required.

30dB Sound Reduction

Multi-layer acoustic foam panels with mass-loaded vinyl barriers. Your conversations stay private — tested and certified at Asia's largest acoustic lab.

Smart Ventilation

Active airflow system continuously circulates fresh air. Designed for sessions lasting hours without stuffiness or CO₂ build-up.

Dimmable LED Lighting

Tunable white LED panels from warm 2700K to cool 6500K. Set the mood for focused work or video calls with perfect lighting.

2× Power Outlets + USB

Built-in power and USB charging keeps your devices running. One external power connection — that's all the Solo needs to operate.

Acoustic Glass Panels

Double-laminated glass maintains visual openness while providing acoustic isolation comparable to a solid wall. Frosted options available.

Fully Customizable

Choose exterior finishes, interior fabrics, glass tints, and accent colours. Custom brand wraps available for corporate deployments.
