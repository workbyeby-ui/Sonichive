What's Inside

Four Seats. Full Team Performance.

Everything the Quartet ships with — engineered for team collaboration at its best.

30dB Sound Reduction

Multi-layer acoustic engineering keeps your team's strategy, ideas, and discussions completely private — no matter how animated the session gets.

Enhanced Ventilation

Four people generate heat. Our enhanced active ventilation system is calibrated for 4-person sessions to maintain comfort for hours.

4× Power + USB

Every seat powered. Four outlets plus USB charging so every team member stays connected without fighting for a single port.

Set the right ambience for brainstorms, presentations, or focused analysis. Dimmable from 2700K to 6500K across the full panel.
